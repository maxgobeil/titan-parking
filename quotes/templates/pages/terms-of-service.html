3. Payment Terms

Payment terms are as follows:

  • For projects under $1,000: Payment in full is due upon completion of the work.
  • For projects over $1,000: A 50% deposit is required before work begins, with the balance due upon completion.
  • Payment methods accepted: Cash, Check, e-Transfer

3.1 Deposits and Payment Schedule

For all jobs valued at $2,000 or more, a 50% deposit is required to secure your place in our schedule. For jobs between $1,000-$2,000, a 30% deposit is required. All deposits must be received at least 5 business days prior to the scheduled start date, or the job may be rescheduled. Deposits are non-refundable within 48 hours of the scheduled service date but may be applied to future services if rescheduling is necessary due to weather conditions.

We will make reasonable efforts to accommodate your schedule when rescheduling is necessary.

4.1 Mobilization and Site Access

Mobilization fees cover the transportation of equipment, materials, and crew to your location for a single scheduled visit. If our crew is unable to complete the work due to circumstances beyond our control (including but not limited to: vehicles parked in work areas, site not being ready as agreed, or client's failure to provide access), a second mobilization fee will be charged for any return visits required to complete the work.

The client is responsible for ensuring that the work area is accessible and clear of vehicles during the scheduled service time. We require that parking lots be at least 90% clear of vehicles during service. Any delay exceeding 30 minutes due to site access issues may result in rescheduling and additional mobilization charges.

5. Surface Preparation

The client is responsible for ensuring that the surface is clean, dry, and in suitable condition for line striping at the time of our scheduled arrival. While we offer sweeping and cleaning services that can be included in your quote, these represent conditions at the time of inspection.

If surface conditions deteriorate between quote acceptance and service date (including but not limited to: new oil spills, mud or dirt accumulation, debris, or construction residue), additional cleaning fees will apply. Our crew will assess the surface condition upon arrival and notify the client of any additional preparation required before proceeding.

Standard cleaning services include light sweeping. Heavy cleaning, power washing, oil spot treatment, or extensive debris removal will incur additional charges based on time and materials required. The client will be notified of these additional charges before work proceeds.
